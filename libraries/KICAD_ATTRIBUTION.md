## Model Categories

The KiCad Spice Library includes models for:

- **Digital Logic**: 74xx, 54xx series ICs
- **Transistors**: BJT, MOSFET, JFET models
- **Diodes**: Standard, Zener, Schottky
- **Operational Amplifiers**: Various manufacturers
- **Manufacturer-Specific**: Texas Instruments, Infineon, Maxim, Linear Technology, and more
- **Passive Components**: Resistors, capacitors, inductors
- **Specialized**: Optocouplers, sensors, power devices

## Usage

These models are automatically indexed and available through the `library_search` MCP tool when SpiceService starts.
